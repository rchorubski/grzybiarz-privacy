  <h1>Privacy Policy – Mushroom Picker App</h1>

  <p>The <strong>Grzybiarz</strong> application does not collect, process, or share any personal data of the user. All data created within the app is stored <strong>exclusively on the user’s device</strong>.</p>

  <h2>Data stored locally</h2>
  <p>The app stores only the data necessary for its functionality:</p>
  <ul>
    <li>mushroom locations added by the user,</li>
    <li>photos taken within the app or added to the gallery,</li>
    <li>application settings (e.g., map mode, view preferences).</li>
  </ul>
  <p>This data is not transmitted to any servers and is not shared with third parties.</p>

  <h2>App permissions</h2>
  <p>The app may use the following device permissions:</p>
  <ul>
    <li><strong>Location</strong> – used only to display the user’s position on the map. Location data is not stored or sent outside the device.</li>
    <li><strong>Camera</strong> – used only for taking photos of mushrooms.</li>
    <li><strong>File access (SAF)</strong> – used only for importing and exporting backups at the user’s request.</li>
    <li><strong>Internet</strong> – used solely for displaying Google Maps and Google AdMob ads. The app does not send any data on its own.</li>
  </ul>

  <h2>Third‑party services</h2>
  <p>The app uses the following Google services:</p>
  <ul>
    <li><strong>Google Maps</strong> – for displaying the map,</li>
    <li><strong>Google AdMob</strong> – for displaying advertisements.</li>
  </ul>
  <p>These services may collect data according to Google’s privacy policy. The app does not have access to data processed by Google.</p>
  <p>Google Privacy Policy: <a href="https://policies.google.com/privacy">https://policies.google.com/privacy</a></p>

  <h2>User control over data</h2>
  <p>The user may at any time:</p>
  <ul>
    <li>delete all saved locations using the <em>“Delete all pins”</em> function,</li>
    <li>remove photos from the gallery,</li>
    <li>uninstall the app, which removes all locally stored data.</li>
  </ul>
  <p>The app does not store any data in the cloud.</p>
